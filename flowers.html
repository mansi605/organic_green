Choose a crop from a list which contains basic Florals.
Steps to follow :-
1. Choose a crop from a list that you are willing to grow.
2. Once clicked, the Ph page will appear.
3. Enter a Ph value of the soil in which a crop will be grown.(to check the Ph of the soil Click on me)
4. click on a check button.
5. The result will be provided on the basis of the soil's Ph to grow a specific crop is high, low or perfect with suitable Organic Fertilzers.

If it show's the Ph is High or Less then you need to provide fertilizers to balance the Ph of the soil.
Do not add more fertilizers it can cause harm to the plants. .
